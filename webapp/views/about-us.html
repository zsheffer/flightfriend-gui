The Flight-Friend Story

As frequent travelers, the last thing we wanted to face after experiencing flight delays, overbookings or a loss in our baggage was a long, painful process trying to get compensation from airlines. We were often frustrated by the lack of transparency of airline conditions of carriage and the hassle and time taken to follow-up with the airline – and we knew we weren’t the only ones.

We believe that the compensation claim process could be made much simpler if only there was an expert that travelers could rely on to help with the entire claims process. We have set about building exactly this.

We strive to give you complete transparency – and, we hope, a peace of mind. We take care of all the troublesome paperwork and time consuming communication with the airlines to strive to help you get compensated. We make it our jobs to acquire the knowledge and expertise to enhance your chances of compensation and expedite the process. We take our job seriously.

Who We Are

We are a team of talented, dedicated individuals currently studying at the Harvard Business School We hail from a variety of different backgrounds (Consulting, Engineering, Investment Management, Venture Capital, Non-profit, Airlines) but are united by a common passion – to create an intuitive service that helps travelers obtain compensation from airlines in a pain-free manner!
