Who's Eligible

Let's have a broad explanation here

Delays

Over 1 in 5 travelers each day is delayed. If it happens to you, we can help you get compensation for your extra expenses.

Cancellations

You’ve just received word that your flight to that important meeting or family event was cancelled—and that it was the airline’s fault. We can help make those “mechanical delays” feel a lot better.

Lost Bags

It’s the worst feeling to be standing at an empty bag belt after all the other passengers have long gone. The airline lost your bag—but they legally owe you up to $3600 in compensation. We can help you get it.

Denied Boarding

The airline overbooked your flight—and you’re the one left standing outside the gate. The law ensures you can get up to the value of your original ticket for your delayed plans. We’ll do all the hard work to make sure you get what you deserve.

Weather

While we can’t stop thunderstorms in Dallas or snow in Chicago, we may be able to help get you some compensation if airlines did not deal with the delays correctly. Please fill out the short question form to help us understand your situation better!

Bad Inflight Experience

Dirty planes. No service. Surly employees. Flying can be terrible—but airlines often provide compensation only when they are taken to court. See our blog for more information on what you can do, and how we can help.
