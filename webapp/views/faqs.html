Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a claim to Flight-Friend?

Submitting a claim is simple: all you need to do is to fill out our short question form in less than 5 minutes on your computer! It is helpful for you to have your original travel booking in hand. You will receive a confirmation of submission on the website, and we will send you an e-mail once your claim is in process.

How much does it cost?

We charge a flat administrative fee of $3 per claim which goes towards covering our claim handling expenses. We also charge a success fee of 15% of the total compensation*. However, if we are not able to get your compensation, no additional fee will not be charged. How long does it take?

The duration of a claim for compensation from the airline varies by claim. Some airlines answer within 2 weeks, others answer within 4-6 weeks and some never answer. We consistently follow up on all claims to get an answer from the airline. To hasten the speed of reply and to increase your chance of compensation, we may also contact the local aviation authorities to lodge a complaint on your behalf.

How can I track my claim status?

After submitting the claim to us you will receive an email update once we have started working on your claim. We will keep you updated if there is a major new development on your claim or if we required additional information. Bear in mind that it could take the airline up to six weeks to answer a claim for compensation. You can also email us at yourflightfriend@gmail.com should you have any questions at any point in time.

Can I do the claim myself?

Yes, everyone can deal directly with their airline to claim compensation for delays. Our Flight-Friend experts, however, have developed expertise in assessing the category which your claim falls into, what you are likely to get compensated and the data required to submit your claim. Our high volume of claims ensures an effective and inexpensive claim handling process enabling us to maximise your chances of compensation. We also dedicate resources to responding to airlines in a timely manner hence expediting the compensation process with airlines.

How far back can I get compensation?

Airlines typically require claims to be submitted no later than 21 days after the incident. We suggest that you submit your claim to us as soon as possible to ensure that you meet the deadlines on the part of the airlines.

For lost luggage claims, please make sure you notify the airline of the claim and obtain a baggage report number no later than 4 hours after arrival of flight or receipt of baggage.
